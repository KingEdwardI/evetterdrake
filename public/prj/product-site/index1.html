About The Games

Beginning in 1987 and continuing on with seemingly endless releases, each game has it's own storyline, but similar play-styles. Join these heros on their epic adventures against evil. Battle treacherous enemies, the forces of evil, and save the world. With seventeen central single player games, two MMORPGs, and dozens more, adding up to thounsands of hours of available game time the adventures never end.
